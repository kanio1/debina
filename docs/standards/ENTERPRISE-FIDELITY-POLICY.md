# Enterprise Fidelity Policy

Debina maximizes enterprise and payment-domain fidelity within its synthetic research-platform boundary. Fidelity means source-backed concepts, realistic failure/recovery and ownership boundaries, observable lifecycle evidence, and explicit rail applicability—not imitation of an inaccessible participant implementation.

Represent business groupings, batches, files, bulks, instructions, submissions, and interchanges only when an authoritative source supports the applicable meaning. Model the smallest construct that preserves the required invariant; a concept is not automatically an aggregate. Keep an explicit `[ASSUMPTION]` with owner, reason, confidence, review trigger, and exit condition whenever evidence is incomplete. Debina makes no certification, legal-settlement, or regulatory-compliance claim.
