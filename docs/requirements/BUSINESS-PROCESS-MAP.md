# Business Process Map

The authoritative process records are in [BUSINESS-PROCESS-CATALOG.yaml](BUSINESS-PROCESS-CATALOG.yaml). They preserve transaction/message/group/batch/file/submission/cycle/position as distinct processing units.

`BP-01 → BP-03 → BP-04 → BP-05 or BP-06 → BP-09`; `BP-07` is an independent inbound message/evidence path. `BP-02`, `BP-08` and `BP-10` require additional source-backed/use-case realization.
