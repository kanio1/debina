# AI Facilitation Limits

An agent may draft flows, examples, traceability and source classifications from repository evidence. It may not claim stakeholder agreement, domain-expert confirmation, participant behavior, a human workshop, or full ATAM. `AI_DRAFT` defaults to `NOT_REVIEWED`; human review evidence must name a durable decision artifact or explicit user decision. Material questions require an owner, source/decision needed and blocking status; they block READY when material.
