## Pułapki, których nie wolno powtórzyć

- **Broad, epic-level `depends_on` references can silently manufacture cycles that a correctly-scoped story-level reference would never create** — demonstrated twice for real in this session (H1: `EPIC-31`/`31.3`↔`EPIC-43`; and incidentally `EPIC-14`/`14.3`↔`EPIC-39`/`39.4`), and a third time as an artifact of the graph-building tooling itself while modeling the (unfixed) `EPIC-14`/`14.1`→`EPIC-46` hidden blocker at whole-epic granularity. Always narrow a cross-epic `depends_on` to the specific story that produces the needed capability, never the whole epic, unless the whole epic genuinely gates the work.
- **An epic reading as "blocked" at the epic level can hide a genuinely `READY` sub-capability** — `EPIC-27` looked blocked on all of `EPIC-26` (which is not `done`, because of one unrelated GraphQL story, `26.4`), when the correlation engine only ever needed `26.1`–`26.3` (done since 2026-07-15). Same for `EPIC-35` vs. `EPIC-12` (only needed `12.1`, not the `[NO-CODE]`-until-Iteration-5 `12.2`). Always check whether an epic's "not `done`" status is actually caused by the specific story you depend on, before assuming it blocks you.
- **A `blocked` story can hide already-`done`, already-verified work inside it** — `EPIC-10`'s old `Story 10.1` read as 100% blocked, but 27 tests across two proof suites (`SET LOCAL ROLE`, `SECURITY DEFINER`) were already passing and permanently committed as test classes. Splitting out the done half (`10.1E`) made this visible; leaving a partially-complete story unsplit hides real progress from anyone scanning `planning/README.md`'s status column.
- Pułapki z poprzednich sesji (pełna historia w `git log -p HANDOFF.md`, jeśli commit tej sesji kiedyś powstanie — ta sesja nic nie commitowała): JPA write-behind pod przełączoną rolą; migracje w tej samej, nie-scommitowanej sesji można resetować; `clean test-compile` gdy Maven incremental ukrywa błąd; cursor-pagination `size()==limit` nie wystarcza; `act`+Testcontainers = zawsze `Could not find a valid Docker environment`, nie regresja; ArchUnit domyślnie skanuje też test-classes, użyj `DO_NOT_INCLUDE_TESTS`; `SECURITY DEFINER` wymaga jawnego `SET search_path` (CVE-2007-2138-class hijacking bez niego) i `USAGE` na schemacie oprócz `EXECUTE` na funkcji; `set_config(..., true)` wraca do `''` (pusty string), nie SQL `NULL`, po commit ORAZ po rollback.
